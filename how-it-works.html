How It Works

Our process is designed to be simple, transparent, and stress-free. From start to finish, you're in control. Here's exactly what to expect.

01

📋 Submit Your Property Information

Fill out our short online form with your property address and basic details. This takes less than 2 minutes — no sign-up required.

We'll use this information to research your property, analyze comparable sales in your area, and prepare a preliminary valuation. You can also call us directly if you prefer to speak with someone.

02

📊 Receive Your Cash Offer

Within 24-48 hours, we'll present you with a fair, no-obligation cash offer based on current market data and your property's condition.

Our offers are transparent — we'll show you exactly how we arrived at the number, including comparable sales data. There are no hidden fees or surprise deductions. The number we present is what you'll receive at closing.

03

📅 Choose Your Closing Date

If you accept our offer, you choose the closing date that works best for you — as quick as 7 days or up to 60 days. You're in control.

Need time to find your next home? No problem. We can work with your schedule. Our team handles all the paperwork, title work, and coordinates with the closing attorney or title company.

04

💵 Get Paid & Move Forward

At closing, you receive your cash. The funds are processed through a licensed title company for your protection. That's it — no hidden steps.

We handle the title transfer, any outstanding liens (with your approval), and ensure everything is done legally and properly. You walk away with cash in hand and complete peace of mind.

Common Questions

How do you determine the offer price?

We analyze recent comparable sales in your area, current market conditions, and the estimated condition of your property. Our offers are below retail market value because we cover all closing costs, make all repairs, and take on the risk. We encourage you to get multiple opinions before deciding.

Can I change my mind after accepting?

Yes. You have the right to review all documents and can withdraw before closing. We believe in informed decisions, not pressure tactics. We recommend having an attorney review the purchase agreement.

Do you buy houses with tenants?

Yes, we can purchase properties with existing tenants. We'll work with you to understand the tenant situation and structure the deal accordingly, always in compliance with local tenant protection laws.

What areas do you serve?

We currently purchase properties across the United States. Some areas have more active buying programs than others. Submit your property information and we'll let you know quickly if we can help.
