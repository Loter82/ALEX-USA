Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 5, 2026

Foreclosure Rescue Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Your Privacy Rights: This policy complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable, and other relevant privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our services:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Property Information: Property address, ownership details, property condition, estimated value
  • Financial Information: Mortgage status, loan balance, foreclosure timeline (we do NOT collect credit card information)
  • Communication Records: Records of your interactions with us, including emails, phone calls, and chat logs

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent on pages, links clicked, referring website
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies (see Section 8)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Property data providers (ATTOM Data, public records)
  • Social media platforms (if you interact with our social media)
  • Marketing partners and lead generation services
  • Credit bureaus (only with your explicit consent)

4. Your Privacy Rights

4.1 Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know: Request disclosure of personal information we collect, use, and share
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to exceptions)
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of the "sale" of personal information (though we don't sell data)
  • Non-Discrimination: Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

4.2 Rights Under GDPR (EEA Residents)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Obtain confirmation of data processing and access your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and fulfill transactions
  • Comply with legal obligations (typically 7 years for real estate records)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Support business operations and analysis

After the retention period, we securely delete or anonymize your information.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure Storage: Industry-standard secure servers and databases
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training

⚠️ Important: No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
